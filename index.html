Bethesda Prayer City

Bethesda Prayer Centre dfgd

It spans 1.5 acres of land at the foothills of the Western Ghats. It is close to Siruvani waterfalls and surrounded by lush green lawns, plants and trees with bright blooming flowers and chirping birds and peacocks.

Bethesda highlights

  • An ideal stay at either our cottages or premier suites offering a comfortable space up to 470 sq.ft., each with lake / lagoon water views.
  • Indulge in the authentic flavours of Kerala along with many Indian and international dishes at The Lakeside restaurant overlooking the Vembanad Lake.
  • With Kerala being the home of ayurvedha, enjoy a holistic experience with age-old treatments and therapies at the Subuthi spa.
  • Enjoy a range of fun activities at the resort like kayaking, shikara rides, jet-ski, sunset cruise, miniature golf, fishing at the lake and many more.
